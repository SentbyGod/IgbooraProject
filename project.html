THE IGBOORA PROJECT

"In the Hands of the Gods" is a thought-provoking and culturally enriching documentary that explores the fascinating phenomenon of twin births in Igboora, Nigeria, which boasts the highest rate of twins in the world. Through intimate and insightful interviews with locals and traditional healers, the film allows the story to be told by the people themselves, delving into the historical and cultural factors behind this phenomenon.
The documentary raises intriguing questions about the role of the gods in blessing people with twins, or whether it is a case of covering curses in a beautiful foil of blessing. Does this blessing of twins truly come from the gods, or is there more to this mystery than meets the eye? This captivating documentary invites viewers to ponder the intricate relationship between science, food, culture, and spirituality in shaping their world and our own.
